Debt and Deficits

Historically, our largest deficits were caused by increased spending around national emergencies like major wars or the Great Depression. Today, our deficits are caused mainly by predictable structural factors: our aging baby boom generation, rising healthcare costs, and a tax system that does not bring in enough money to pay for what the government has promised its citizens.
